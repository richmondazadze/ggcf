Who We Are

Good God Charity Foundation

Good God Charity Foundation is a Non-profit Charitable Foundation founded in 2022 with a dedication to improving child health and education in Ghana and beyond. Through healthcare programs, educational support, and community outreach, we aim to raise awareness, increase donations, and foster community involvement in underrepresented areas. Our Impact

Orphanage & Child Support

We bring practical help and hope to vulnerable children and families. Through multiple donation drives, we've supplied food, clothing, and hygiene essentials to orphanages like Kumasi Children's Home, and to children in need. Each visit brings healing, dignity, and the love of Christ in action.

Learn More

Educational Empowerment

From books to mentorship, we're building brighter futures. GGCF has supported students in under-resourced schools like New Amakom with learning materials, menstrual hygiene packs, and school fees support. Our programs focus on breaking the cycle of poverty through access to education.

Learn More

Child & Maternal Healthcare Relief

We stand in the gap where medical help is out of reach. Through donations to hospitals like Komfo Anokye Teaching Hospital, we've provided thermometers, blood pressure monitors, and other life-saving equipment. We ensure children and mothers receive the care they deserve—because health is hope.
