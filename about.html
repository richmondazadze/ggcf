About Us

Good God Charity Foundation

Founded by Theophilus Mensah in 2022, the Good God Charity Foundation is a dedicated and compassionate non-profit organization based in Kumasi, Ghana. We are committed to supporting child healthcare, education, and bringing hope to vulnerable communities. Our foundation is built on the belief that no act of kindness is ever wasted, and we strive to make a meaningful impact by assisting those in need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to give hope to those who need it most. We are committed to addressing the needs of children, students, orphanages, schools, and abandoned pregnant women in hospitals. Our foundation aims to improve the lives of children and underrepresented families by providing vital support in healthcare and education.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every child has access to the essential resources they need to thrive. Our vision is to bring hope to those in low-income and underrepresented families by providing consistent support that fosters their well-being. Whether it's providing educational materials, healthcare equipment, or basic life necessities, we believe our efforts will create a ripple effect that uplifts entire communities.

Who We Serve

The Good God Charity Foundation targets orphanages, schools, and hospitals in need of critical support. We assist abandoned pregnant women who lack the means to care for themselves and their unborn babies, as well as orphans and children in need of clothing, food, and educational materials. Our focus extends to supporting low-income families who require assistance with healthcare, schooling, and daily living.

Our Impact

Stories of Hope and Change

December 2022

Our First Donation: Christmas at the Orphanage

After completing high school, my close friends and I—a small group of fewer than 15—wanted to express our gratitude to God for seeing us through successfully. Instead of a typical celebration, we felt compelled to give back and decided to celebrate Christmas by visiting the Kumasi Children's Home. Despite our small size, we were determined to make a difference and worked together to gather enough funds and resources to make it happen.

With hearts full of generosity, we donated food items, clothes, washing soap, powder, detergents, toothbrushes, and other essential supplies, along with a financial contribution. The orphanage, which cares for around 120 children, welcomed us with open arms and immense gratitude. It was a deeply rewarding experience to see the joy on the children's faces, knowing that our efforts brought them happiness during the Christmas season. This marked the beginning of what is now the Good God Charity Foundation, rooted in the spirit of giving back and making a meaningful impact in our community.

December 2023

Second Visit to Kumasi Children's Home (2023)

In 2023, we once again had the privilege of supporting the Kumasi Children's Home by donating a variety of essential items, including food, clothes, toiletries, and much more. This marked our second visit to the home, as we had previously visited and donated in 2022.

This time, we celebrated Christmas with the children, sharing in the festive joy and bringing a little extra warmth to their hearts during the holiday season. It was an incredibly fulfilling experience to see their smiles and happiness as they received the gifts. Our team felt blessed to be able to brighten their day and create lasting memories with these wonderful children. The moments we spent together served as a beautiful reminder of the impact of giving and the importance of showing love and care to those in need.

December 2023

Supporting Maternal and Child Health (2023)

In December 2023, we reached out to the Maternal and Child Health Hospital in Kumasi, Adum-Pampaso in Ghana to assess their most pressing needs for pregnant women, unborn babies, and newborns. The hospital provided us with a list of essential medical equipment that was urgently required to improve the care provided to mothers and babies. They specifically requested vital items, including blood pressure (BP) apparatus, thermometers, a scale, and an ambulance bag.

By God's grace and through your generous support, we were not only able to fulfill their entire request, but we also went above and beyond by providing additional supplies. In total, we donated all the requested equipment plus three extra thermometers to further support their efforts. Moreover, recognizing the daily needs of the pregnant women at the hospital, we supplemented our donation with small bags of rice, wipers, soap, toilet rolls, and washing powder to help ease their personal challenges during their stay.

When we delivered the items, the hospital staff warmly welcomed us and expressed their deep appreciation for the timely support. They conveyed how urgently these items were needed to ensure safe deliveries and proper care for the women and babies. The gratitude and relief in their voices showed how meaningful our contributions were in addressing their immediate needs. The entire experience reminded us all of the power of community and how much of a difference we can make when we come together for a good cause.
